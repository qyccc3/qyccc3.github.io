Alternate Canvas

Project Overview
The Mathematics and Computer Science (MathCS) Department wants to hire you to create a web application to replace Canvas, our learning management system. The learning management system will allow students and faculty to manage grades and discussion questions seamlessly (think of this as simplified Canvas with Piazza support integrated seamlessly).
Detailed Description
This website allows three different kinds of users to login in.
Teacher Login Page
Student Login Page
TA Login Page
Main Page For Teachers and Teaching Assistants
Main Page For Students
Course Content For Teachers and Teaching Assistants
Course Content For Students
Teachers and Teaching Assistants are allowed to see all the students who are enrolled in the specific class. They are also allowed to search a specific assignment and edit it or create new assignments. They are also allowed to see the final grade of all the enrolled students. Students on the other hand are only allowed to see the assignments the teacher has assigned to them. Post is a function that is available to all the users.
